## Country-Specific Insights

### Tier 1 Markets (Highest Opportunity)
- **Brazil** — 99% WhatsApp penetration, PIX payments, 78% businesses on WA
- **Mexico** — 93% WA penetration, OXXO/SPEI payments, Stripe +139% growth
- **Colombia** — 94% WA penetration, PSE/Efecty payments, growing digital economy

### Tier 2 Markets
- **Argentina** — 90% WA, Mercado Pago dominant, inflation-aware pricing needed
- **Chile** — Webpay dominant, strong digital adoption
- **Peru** — PagoEfectivo, growing e-commerce

### Tier 3 Markets (Home Base)
- **Panama** — Yappy/BAC payments, DGI e-invoicing mandate 2026, Kenneth's home market

## Key Success Factors for LatAm SMB Tools
1. **WhatsApp-native** — Not an add-on, the primary channel
2. **Spanish-first** — UI, AI responses, documentation all in Spanish
3. **Local payment methods** — Not just credit cards
4. **Mobile-first** — 75% of commerce is mobile
5. **Low-cost entry** — Free tier with real value (workspace)
6. **Fast time-to-value** — <10 minutes to first value or you lose them
7. **AI that saves time** — Automate the repetitive, advise on the strategic
