# Panama Compliance & E-Invoicing SOP v1

**Owner:** COO Agent
**Type:** compliance

## Summary
Panama's DGI (Direccion General de Ingresos) is tightening e-invoicing requirements effective January 1, 2026. Every Kitz user in Panama must understand these changes.

## Critical Changes (Resolution No. 201-6299, July 2025)

### Free Invoicing System (Facturador Gratuito) — New Limits
Effective **January 1, 2026**, the free e-invoicing tool is restricted to:
- Annual invoicing up to **B/. 36,000**
- Maximum **100 electronic invoices per month**

### Mandatory Migration
Taxpayers exceeding these limits MUST:
1. Migrate to a **PAC (Proveedor Autorizado Calificado)** — Qualified Authorized Provider
2. OR implement their own **DGI-certified e-invoicing system**

### Non-Compliance Penalties
- Fines
- Rejection of invoices
- Other sanctions impacting business operations

## Requirements for E-Invoicing Compliance
1. **RUC** — Registered and updated with DGI
2. **Qualified electronic signature** — Certificate from a certified provider
3. **SFEP registration** — Registered as authorized user in the electronic invoicing system
4. **XML compliance** — Documents must follow current technical specification (structure, fields, validations)

## Kitz Action Items
1. **Alert Panama users** approaching B/. 36,000 annual invoicing or 100 invoices/month
2. **Track invoice counts** in workspace — warn at 80% threshold
3. **Recommend PAC migration** for businesses exceeding limits
4. **Generate DGI-compliant XML** for e-invoices when possible
5. **Store RUC** in customer profile for compliance tracking

## Payment Methods (Panama-Specific)
- **Yappy** — Real-time P2P/P2B payments (Banco General)
- **BAC** — Bank transfers and card processing
- **Nequi** — Mobile wallet
- **Cash vouchers** — Still common for online purchases
- **Credit/Debit cards** — Growing but not universal

## Rules
- Never issue invoices without proper RUC validation
- Track monthly invoice count per user — compliance is non-negotiable
- Warn users 30 days before they hit free invoicing limits
- Spanish only for all compliance-related communications
- Keep DGI regulation links updated — check quarterly
