# Order Fulfillment SOP v1

**Owner:** COO Agent
**Type:** business

## Summary
Confirm the order, pack it, ship it, track it, follow up.

## Steps
1. **Confirm order** — Verify payment received. Send confirmation via WhatsApp.
2. **Prepare order** — Mark as "processing" in system. Notify warehouse/team.
3. **Ship it** — Update tracking info. Mark as "shipped" in system.
4. **Send tracking** — WhatsApp the customer their tracking link.
5. **Follow up** — 48 hours after delivery, check in: "Everything good?"
6. **Close order** — Mark as "fulfilled" once confirmed received.

## Rules
- No shipping without confirmed payment
- Always send tracking within 2 hours of shipping
- Follow-up is mandatory — it's how you get repeat customers
