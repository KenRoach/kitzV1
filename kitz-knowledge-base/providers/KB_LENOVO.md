# Lenovo — OEM & Tech Provider Knowledge Base

## Company Overview
- **Founded**: 1984 as Legend Holdings (Beijing, China). Acquired IBM PC Division 2005
- **Ticker**: 0992 (HKSE)
- **Revenue**: ~$62B (FY2024/25)
- **HQ**: Beijing, China + Morrisville, NC (dual HQ)
- **CEO**: Yuanqing Yang
- **Employees**: ~77,000
- **Market position**: World's #1 or #2 PC vendor (swaps with HP quarterly), #3 server vendor globally

## Business Segments

### Intelligent Devices Group (IDG)
- **ThinkPad**: Flagship business laptops (T/X/L/E series, X1 Carbon, X1 Yoga, X1 Nano)
- **ThinkBook**: SMB-focused laptops (affordable, modern design)
- **ThinkCentre**: Business desktops (Tiny, SFF, Tower)
- **ThinkStation**: Workstations (P-series towers, P-series mobile)
- **ThinkVision**: Business monitors (USB-C docking, color-accurate)
- **IdeaPad / IdeaCentre**: Consumer laptops & desktops
- **Legion**: Gaming PCs
- **Yoga**: Premium consumer 2-in-1s
- **Tablets**: Tab P, Tab M, ThinkPad X1 Fold

### Infrastructure Solutions Group (ISG)
- **ThinkSystem**: Rack & tower servers (SR/ST series). Intel Xeon / AMD EPYC
- **ThinkAgile**: HCI (VX with VMware vSAN, HX with Nutanix, MX with Azure Stack HCI)
- **ThinkEdge**: Edge servers (compact, ruggedized)
- **Storage**: ThinkSystem DM (NetApp ONTAP-based), ThinkSystem DE (E-series)
- **Networking**: RackSwitch, Flex System switches (campus/DC)
- **GPU servers**: ThinkSystem SR675 V3 (8x NVIDIA H100), ThinkSystem SR685a V3

### Solutions & Services Group (SSG)
- **Lenovo TruScale**: Infrastructure-as-a-Service (compute, storage, HCI, DaaS)
- **Managed Services**: Device management, security, help desk
- **Lenovo Premier Support**: Dedicated 24/7 engineer, next-business-day on-site
- **Deployment Services**: Asset tagging, BIOS config, imaging, Smart Deploy
- **Sustainability Services**: CO2 offset, asset recovery, packaging take-back

## SMB Programs & Relevance
- **Lenovo Pro for Small Business**: lenovo.com/us/en/lenovopro — exclusive discounts (up to 5% back in rewards), free membership
- **ThinkBook line**: Designed specifically for SMBs — modern design, competitive pricing, business features without enterprise overhead
- **Lenovo TruScale DaaS**: Pay-per-seat PC management — includes device, support, lifecycle management
- **Lenovo Vantage**: Device management + health diagnostics app (consumer + commercial)
- **Lenovo Device Intelligence**: AI-powered fleet analytics (battery health, crash prediction, app performance)

## Partner Program (Lenovo Partner Hub / Lenovo 360)
- **Tiers**: Authorized, Silver, Gold, Platinum
- **Lenovo 360**: Unified partner framework across PC, server, services (launched 2022)
- **Benefits**: Deal registration, MDF, rebates, training (Lenovo Expert Achievers Program)
- **Specializations**: Data Center, HCI, Workstations, Services
- **Distribution**: Ingram Micro, TD SYNNEX, D&H, ScanSource

## LatAm Presence
- **Regional operations**: Sao Paulo (Brazil HQ), Mexico City, Buenos Aires, Santiago, Bogota, Lima
- **Manufacturing**: Monterrey, Mexico (PCs for Americas). Itu/Indaiatuba, Brazil
- **Strong position**: Lenovo is one of the top PC brands in LatAm, particularly in Brazil, Mexico, Argentina
- **SMB focus**: ThinkBook and IdeaPad lines popular with cost-conscious LatAm SMBs
- **Channel**: Heavy distributor/reseller model in LatAm markets
- **Language support**: Full Spanish/Portuguese Vantage app, local-language support lines

## APIs & Integration Points
- **Lenovo XClarity Administrator API**: REST API for server fleet management (Redfish-compliant)
- **XClarity Integrator**: Plugins for VMware vCenter, Microsoft SCCM, Nutanix Prism
- **Lenovo Device Manager API**: Cloud-based fleet management for PCs
- **Lenovo Vantage Enterprise API**: Policy management, BIOS settings, driver updates
- **ThinkShield API**: Endpoint security policy management
- **Lenovo TruScale API**: Consumption metering, provisioning for as-a-service

- **ThinkShield**: End-to-end security platform (BIOS, firmware, OS, identity, network)
- **Self-Healing BIOS**: Automatic BIOS recovery from corruption
- **ThinkShield Hardware Password Manager**: Centralized BIOS password management
- **Absolute Persistence**: Firmware-embedded endpoint visibility (optional)
- **Supply chain**: Transparent supply chain program, component-level verification
- **Compliance**: FIPS 140-2, TAA-compliant models, Common Criteria options
