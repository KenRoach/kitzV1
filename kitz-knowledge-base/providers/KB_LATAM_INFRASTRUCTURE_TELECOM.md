## LatAm Cloud & Data Center Providers

### DigitalOcean (LatAm-relevant)
- **Not LatAm-headquartered** but extremely popular with LatAm developers/SMBs
- **Why LatAm loves DO**: Simple pricing, $4/mo Droplets, managed databases, App Platform
- **No LatAm data center** but NYC/SFO regions serve LatAm with low latency
- **Pricing**: Droplets from $4/mo, Managed PostgreSQL from $15/mo, App Platform from $5/mo

### Huawei Cloud (LatAm)
- **Huawei LatAm HQ**: Mexico City
- **Cloud regions**: Santiago (Chile), Mexico City, Sao Paulo (Brazil)
- **SMB Play**: Aggressive pricing (often 30-50% cheaper than AWS/Azure in LatAm)
- **Products**: ECS (VMs), OBS (object storage), RDS, AI (ModelArts)
- **Huawei devices**: Massive smartphone market share in some LatAm countries (despite US sanctions)
- **Enterprise networking**: Switches, APs, firewalls — competing with Cisco/Fortinet in LatAm

### Equinix (LatAm Data Centers)
- **Ticker**: EQIX (NASDAQ) | **Revenue**: ~$8.5B (global)
- **LatAm locations**: Sao Paulo (6 sites), Rio de Janeiro, Bogota, Mexico City
- **Services**: Colocation, interconnection, Equinix Metal (bare metal), Equinix Fabric
- **SMB Relevance**: Primarily enterprise, but Equinix Metal offers on-demand bare metal from $0.50/hr

### Ascenty (LatAm Data Centers)
- **Owner**: DigitalBridge + Brookfield
- **Countries**: Brazil (22+ data centers), Chile, Mexico, Colombia
- **Market position**: Largest data center operator in LatAm
- **Services**: Colocation, managed hosting, cloud connectivity

### KIO Networks (Mexico)
- **Founded**: 2002 (Mexico City)
- **Countries**: Mexico, Guatemala, Dominican Republic, Panama, Colombia, Spain
- **Services**: Data centers (35+ sites), managed cloud, cybersecurity, IT outsourcing
- **SMB Relevance**: Managed IT services for Mexican mid-market and SMBs

### Odata (Brazil)
- **Founded**: 2015 (Sao Paulo)
- **Owner**: Aligned Data Centers (Macquarie)
- **Focus**: Hyperscale data centers in Brazil, Colombia, Mexico
- **Clients**: Major cloud providers use Odata facilities in LatAm
