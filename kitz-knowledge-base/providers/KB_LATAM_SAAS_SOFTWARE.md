## TOTVS (Brazil)

### Company Overview
- **Founded**: 1983 (Sao Paulo, Brazil)
- **Ticker**: TOTS3 (B3 — Brazilian stock exchange)
- **Revenue**: ~$4.5B BRL (~$900M USD, FY2024)
- **CEO**: Dennis Herszkowicz
- **Employees**: ~10,000
- **Market position**: #1 ERP in LatAm, #1 tech company in Brazil (by revenue). Largest business software company in emerging markets

### Key Products
- **TOTVS Protheus**: Flagship ERP for mid-market (financials, HR, manufacturing, logistics, retail)
- **TOTVS RM**: ERP for services, education, government
- **TOTVS Datasul**: ERP for manufacturing/distribution
- **TOTVS Fluig**: Low-code platform (workflow, BPM, portals, ECM)
- **TOTVS Techfin**: Joint venture with Itau bank — credit, payments, BNPL for TOTVS customers
- **TOTVS RD Station** (acquired): #1 marketing automation in Brazil
  - RD Station Marketing: Email, landing pages, lead scoring, automation
  - RD Station CRM: Free CRM for SMBs
  - RD Station Conversas: WhatsApp Business management
- **TOTVS Carol**: AI/data platform
- **TOTVS Hospitality**: Hotel PMS, restaurant POS (acquired Bematech)
- **TOTVS Eleve**: Personal finance app

### SMB Relevance
- **RD Station**: Purpose-built for Brazilian/LatAm SMBs
  - RD Station Marketing: From R$50/mo (~$10) — email marketing, landing pages, automation
  - RD Station CRM: Free plan available
  - RD Station Conversas: WhatsApp Business management from R$79/mo
- **TOTVS Micro/Small**: Simplified ERP packages for smaller businesses
- **TOTVS Techfin**: Credit lines for TOTVS customers based on ERP data
- **Massive ecosystem**: 52,000+ customers, 350+ franchise units across Brazil

### APIs
- **TOTVS REST APIs**: Standard REST for all ERP modules (Protheus, RM, Datasul)
- **TOTVS Fluig API**: Workflow, document, identity APIs
- **RD Station API**: REST API for leads, contacts, automations, events
- **TOTVS Carol API**: Data lake, connectors, AI model management

### LatAm: Brazil-dominant (95%+ revenue). Expanding to Mexico, Argentina, Colombia
