## Platform

### Typeform
- **Priority:** Medium
- **ID:** PKB-185
- **URL:** https://www.typeform.com/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** Beautiful conversational forms for leads & surveys

**Extracted Intelligence:**

Conversational one-question-at-a-time form builder. Free: unlimited forms, 10 responses/mo. Basic $29/mo: 100 responses. Plus $50/mo: 3 users, 1,000 responses. Business $89/mo: 10,000 responses. 3.5x higher completion rates than standard forms. Branching logic, AI question generation, Brand Kits. Integrates: Salesforce, Sheets, Slack, Mailchimp, Klaviyo, Stripe, Pipedrive, WordPress.

**KITZ Application:** Ideal for lead gen and surveys. Conversational format aligns with LATAM interactive communication preference. Free tier limited but sufficient for testing.

### Tally (Free Forms)
- **Priority:** Medium
- **ID:** PKB-186
- **URL:** https://tally.so/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** Free-tier form builder (Notion-like simplicity)

**Extracted Intelligence:**

Notion-inspired form builder. Free: unlimited forms AND submissions (fair use), conditional logic, calculator, answer piping, file uploads, signatures, Stripe payments (5% commission). Pro $25/mo: custom domains, no branding, teams. GDPR compliant, hosted in Europe, no cookies, encrypted data.

**KITZ Application:** Best free form solution for LATAM SMBs. Unlimited at $0. Strong recommendation over Typeform for cost-conscious businesses. Payment collection via Stripe enables checkout forms.
