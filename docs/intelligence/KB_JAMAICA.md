## Tax

### TAJ -- Tax Administration Jamaica
- **Priority:** High
- **ID:** PKB-497
- **URL:** https://www.jamaicatax.gov.jm/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** Jamaica tax authority: TRN registration, GCT (15%), income tax, payroll (PAYE)

**Extracted Intelligence:**

TAJ is a revenue authority handling compliance, collection, audit, and assessment.

**Key Tax Types:**
- **GCT (General Consumption Tax):** Filed electronically through SURI portal. Also administers Special Consumption Tax (SCT), Telephone Call Tax (TCT), Guest Accommodation Room Tax (TGART).
- **Income Tax / PAYE:** Withheld from emoluments. Annual return due March 17, 2025 (March 15 fell on weekend). Form: Annual Income Tax Returns for 2024 + Estimated Income for 2025.
- **TRN (Taxpayer Registration Number):** Required for all tax activities.

**Digital Services:** Electronic tax system for filing returns and remitting taxes online. Customer Care Centre: 888-TAX-HELP (888-829-4357).

**KITZ Application:** TRN registration is first step for any Jamaica business. KITZ must track GCT filing deadlines (monthly/quarterly). PAYE withholding obligations critical for employers.

## Registration

### Companies Office of Jamaica
- **Priority:** High
- **ID:** PKB-498
- **URL:** https://www.orcjamaica.com/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** Jamaica company registration: incorporation, annual returns, business names

**Extracted Intelligence:**

COJ (Companies Office of Jamaica) registers local and overseas companies under Ministry of Industry, Investment and Commerce.

**Incorporation Process:** Complete Form BRF1, Form 1A (or 1B), BOR Form A (or B). Fee: J$27,500. Name search available, reserve for 90 days. Head Office: 1 Grenada Way, Kingston 5. Tel: 1 (876) 908-4419/26. Email: custsupport@orcjamaica.com.

**Annual Returns:** All registered companies must file annual returns to notify ownership/management changes.

**KITZ Application:** Guide users through incorporation forms. Track annual return filing deadlines. Name search/reservation is important first step.

## Payments

### Lynk -- Jamaica Mobile Wallet
- **Priority:** Medium
- **ID:** PKB-499
- **URL:** https://lynk.world/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** Jamaica digital wallet: P2P, merchant payments, bill pay, remittances

**Extracted Intelligence:**

Jamaica's largest and fastest growing digital wallet (launched late 2021). Mobile-first, pays from wallet balance or linked bank account.

**Features:**
- **P2P:** Instant transfers using recipient name or QR code, all day every day.
- **Merchants:** QR scan-to-pay at 4,000+ participating merchants (in-store and online).
- **Bill Pay:** Payments to 300+ billers, mobile credit top-ups.
- **Remittances:** Western Union and MoneyGram integration, receive funds directly in app. Lynk/MoneyGram: up to $100,000/day.
- **Security:** Facial recognition + PIN for transactions.
- **Signup:** Under 5 minutes, needs smartphone + valid National ID. No bank account required.

**KITZ Application:** Primary digital payment recommendation for Jamaica. No bank account requirement lowers barrier for unbanked/underbanked. Remittance integration critical for Jamaica (large diaspora). QR payments enable small merchant digitization.
