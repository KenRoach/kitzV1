```
Direccion General de Ingresos (DGI) - Republica de Nicaragua

DEEP ENRICHMENT:

DGI (Direccion General de Ingresos) is Nicaragua's national tax authority, under the
Ministry of Finance (MHCP). It administers all internal revenue taxes.

KEY FUNCTIONS FOR BUSINESSES:

1. RUC (Registro Unico del Contribuyente):
   - Nicaragua's unique taxpayer ID number
   - Required for ALL business activities, invoicing, banking
   - Types: Persona Natural or Persona Juridica
   - Registration at DGI offices with required documentation

2. Tax Types:
   a) IVA (Impuesto al Valor Agregado -- VAT): 15%
      - Exempt: Basic food basket, medicines, education, agricultural inputs
      - Monthly filing and payment (by the 15th)

   b) IR (Impuesto sobre la Renta -- Income Tax):
      - Corporate rate: 30% on net income
      - Alternative minimum: 1% on gross income (whichever is higher)
      - Individuals: Progressive 0-30%
      - Annual filing with monthly advances

   c) Withholding Tax (Retencion en la Fuente):
      - Purchases: 2%, Professional services: 10%, Rent: 10%
      - Monthly filing by the 5th

   d) IMI (Municipal Tax): 1-2% on gross monthly income
   e) IBI (Property Tax): 1% of assessed value annually

3. Regimen de Cuota Fija:
   - Simplified regime for small taxpayers (income < C$100,000/month)
   - Fixed monthly fee replaces IVA + IR
   - IDEAL for micro/small businesses (KITZ target users)
   - Cannot issue IVA-bearing invoices

4. Digital Services:
   - VET (Ventanilla Electronica Tributaria): Online tax portal
   - Electronic declarations and payments
   - RUC consultation, tax calendar, constancias

KITZ RELEVANCE:
- Essential for Nicaraguan KITZ users
- Cuota Fija is the most common regime for small businesses
- 30% corporate rate + 1% minimum tax are key planning factors
- Tax calendar reminders prevent costly penalties
- Less digitized than Colombia or Argentina
```
