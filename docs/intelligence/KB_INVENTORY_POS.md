## Platform

### Square
- **Priority:** High
- **ID:** PKB-179
- **URL:** https://squareup.com/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** POS + payments + inventory for retail & food

**Extracted Intelligence:**

**Pricing Plans:**
- Free Plan ($0/mo): single-location sellers and startups.
- Square Plus ($29/mo/location): staff management, inventory reports.
- Square Premium ($79-149/mo/location): custom pricing, advanced tools, 2.4% + 15c in-person.

**Hardware:** Square Stand (iPad register, boutiques/cafes), Square Terminal ($299, portable all-in-one), Square Register (dual-screen, high-volume).

**Restaurant Features:** Payment processing, menu management, labor/inventory cost management, smart KDS (2025), order management.

**Retail Features:** Advanced inventory management, vendor/purchase order management, shift management, inventory reporting.

**Payments:** Credit cards, Google Pay, Apple Pay. Real-time inventory sync, AI-powered demand forecasts, omnichannel sales sync (in-person + online + delivery).

**KITZ Application:** Strong recommendation for LATAM retail and food businesses. Free tier accessible to startups. AI-powered forecasts help with inventory optimization. Omnichannel sync critical for businesses selling across multiple channels.

### Loyverse (Free POS)
- **Priority:** High
- **ID:** PKB-180
- **URL:** https://loyverse.com/
- **Why KITZ needs it:** Free POS system for small retail & restaurants

**Extracted Intelligence:**

Cloud-based, mobile POS for small retail, restaurants, and salons.

**Free Features:** Multi-store management, basic inventory tracking, sales reporting, built-in loyalty program, weighted barcodes, bulk inventory, bottle deposits, stock counting. Sell from iOS/Android smartphones/tablets. Offline mode (stores and syncs data when reconnected). Discounts, order modifications, refunds. Printed/electronic receipts.

**Paid Add-ons:** Unlimited Sales History, Employee Management, Advanced Inventory. Starts at $5/mo per add-on, 14-day free trial.

**User Profile:** 46% of reviewers are small business POS users. Strong value-for-money ratings.

**KITZ Application:** Best free POS for LATAM small businesses. Mobile-first design perfect for informal sellers. Offline mode critical in areas with unreliable internet. Built-in loyalty program adds retention capability at zero cost. Strong recommendation for micro-businesses not ready for Square's ecosystem.
