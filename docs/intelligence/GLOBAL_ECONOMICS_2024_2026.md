### Expansion Decision Criteria

For each new market, Kitz agents should evaluate:

1. **WhatsApp penetration** — >80% = Kitz-compatible
2. **Informal economy size** — Higher = larger addressable market
3. **Digital payment infrastructure** — Instant payments = transaction enabler
4. **Language** — Spanish first, then Portuguese, then English
5. **Regulatory complexity** — Simpler = faster launch
6. **SMB density** — More microenterprises = more potential users
7. **VC/funding ecosystem** — Capital availability for local operations
8. **Cultural proximity** — Similar business patterns to LatAm
