## Panama

### Requirements
1. **Business Name Registration** — Registro Público de Panamá
2. **RUC (Registro Único de Contribuyente)** — Tax ID from DGI (dgi.mef.gob.pa)
3. **Aviso de Operación** — Operating notice via Panamá Emprende (panamaemprende.gob.pa)
4. **Municipal License** — From Municipio de Panamá (mupa.gob.pa)
5. **CSS Registration** — Social security for employers (css.gob.pa)
6. **Commercial License** — MICI (mici.gob.pa) for regulated activities
7. **Bank Account** — Banco General, BAC, Banistmo (requires RUC + Aviso)

### Business Structures
- **Empresa Individual** — Sole proprietor (simplest)
- **SRL (Sociedad de Responsabilidad Limitada)** — LLC equivalent
- **SA (Sociedad Anónima)** — Corporation (most common for foreign investors)

### Costs
- Aviso de Operación: Free via Panamá Emprende
- Municipal license: $50-$500 depending on activity
- Legal fees for SA: $1,000-$2,000
- Timeline: 1-5 business days via Panamá Emprende

### Tax Obligations
- **ITBMS**: 7% VAT on goods and services
- **Income Tax**: 25% corporate, 0% on foreign-sourced income
- **CSS**: Employer 12.25% + Employee 9.75% of payroll
- **13th Month (Décimo Tercer Mes)**: Mandatory, paid in 3 installments

## Mexico

### Requirements
1. **RFC (Registro Federal de Contribuyentes)** — Tax ID from SAT (sat.gob.mx)
2. **e.firma (Electronic Signature)** — Required for all tax procedures
3. **SIGER Registration** — Business registration portal (gob.mx/tuempresa)
4. **IMSS Registration** — Social security for employees (imss.gob.mx)
5. **Municipal License** — From local municipality
6. **Environmental License** — SEMARNAT if applicable
7. **Health Permits** — COFEPRIS for food, health, cosmetics businesses

### Business Structures
- **Persona Física con Actividad Empresarial** — Sole proprietor
- **RESICO (Régimen Simplificado de Confianza)** — Simplified regime for small businesses (< MX$3.5M annual)
- **SAS (Sociedad por Acciones Simplificada)** — Simplified corporation (online, free, < MX$5M annual)
- **SA de CV** — Traditional corporation

### Costs
- RFC: Free
- SAS: Free (online via gob.mx)
- SA de CV: MX$5,000-$15,000 legal fees
- Municipal license: Varies by municipality

### Tax Obligations
- **IVA**: 16% VAT
- **ISR**: Income tax (progressive rates for individuals, 30% corporate)
- **CFDI**: Electronic invoicing mandatory for all transactions
- **IMSS**: Employer contributions 20-35% of payroll

## Brazil

### Requirements
1. **CNPJ (Cadastro Nacional da Pessoa Jurídica)** — Federal tax ID
2. **State Registration (Inscrição Estadual)** — For ICMS taxpayers
3. **Municipal Registration (Inscrição Municipal)** — For ISS taxpayers
4. **Alvará de Funcionamento** — Operating license from municipality
5. **INSS Registration** — Social security
6. **FGTS Registration** — Employee severance fund (Caixa Econômica Federal)
7. **Environmental License** — If applicable (IBAMA)
8. **Sanitary License** — ANVISA for food, health businesses

### Business Structures
- **MEI (Microempreendedor Individual)** — Micro-entrepreneur (< R$81K/year, 1 employee max)
- **ME (Microempresa)** — Micro-enterprise (< R$360K/year)
- **EPP (Empresa de Pequeno Porte)** — Small company (< R$4.8M/year)
- **Ltda (Sociedade Limitada)** — LLC equivalent
- **SA (Sociedade Anônima)** — Corporation

### Costs
- MEI: R$71.60/month (fixed tax — covers federal + state + municipal)
- ME/EPP Simples Nacional: 4-33% based on revenue tier
- Ltda formation: R$1,500-$5,000 legal fees

### Tax Obligations
- **Simples Nacional**: Unified tax (4-33% of revenue, covers 8 taxes)
- **ICMS**: State VAT (7-25%)
- **ISS**: Municipal service tax (2-5%)
- **NF-e**: Electronic invoice mandatory
- **PIX**: No fees for individuals, < 1% for merchants

## Colombia

### Requirements
1. **RUT (Registro Único Tributario)** — Tax ID from DIAN (dian.gov.co)
2. **NIT (Número de Identificación Tributaria)** — Business tax number
3. **Cámara de Comercio Registration** — Chamber of commerce
4. **Municipal Registration** — Industry & commerce tax (ICA)
5. **ARL Registration** — Workplace risk insurance
6. **EPS & AFP Registration** — Health and pension for employees
7. **INVIMA Registration** — For food, pharma, cosmetics

### Business Structures
- **Persona Natural** — Sole proprietor
- **SAS (Sociedad por Acciones Simplificada)** — Most popular (flexible, online creation)
- **Ltda** — LLC equivalent
- **SA** — Corporation

### Costs
- SAS: COP $200,000-$500,000 (~$50-$125 USD)
- Chamber of commerce annual renewal: COP $100,000-$1,000,000 based on assets
- Timeline: 1-3 days for SAS

### Tax Obligations
- **IVA**: 19% VAT
- **Renta**: 35% corporate income tax
- **ICA**: Municipal industry & commerce tax (varies by city, 0.2-1.4%)
- **Electronic invoicing**: Mandatory via DIAN

## Argentina

### Requirements
1. **CUIT (Clave Única de Identificación Tributaria)** — Tax ID from ARCA/AFIP
2. **Monotributo or Responsable Inscripto** — Tax regime registration
3. **Habilitación Municipal** — Municipal operating permit
4. **ART Registration** — Workplace risk insurance
5. **Obra Social** — Health coverage for employees
6. **IIBB (Ingresos Brutos)** — Provincial gross income tax registration
7. **Bank Account** — Requires CUIT

### Business Structures
- **Monotributista** — Simplified regime (< ARS annual limit, ~$50K USD)
- **SAS** — Simplified corporation (created online, 24-hour registration)
- **SRL** — LLC equivalent
- **SA** — Corporation

### Costs
- Monotributo: ARS $5,000-$50,000/month (category-based, includes tax + health + pension)
- SAS formation: ARS $50,000-$150,000
- Timeline: SAS can be formed in 24 hours online

### Tax Obligations
- **IVA**: 21% VAT (10.5% reduced rate for certain goods)
- **Ganancias**: Income tax (progressive for individuals, 35% corporate)
- **IIBB**: Provincial gross income tax (1-5%)
- **Monotributo**: Fixed monthly payment covering all taxes (for qualifying small businesses)

## Chile

### Requirements
1. **Inicio de Actividades** — Tax registration with SII (sii.cl)
2. **RUT** — Tax ID (assigned automatically to Chileans)
3. **Patente Municipal** — Municipal operating permit
4. **AFP Registration** — Pension fund for employees
5. **Isapre/Fonasa** — Health insurance registration
6. **Mutual de Seguridad** — Workplace accident insurance

### Business Structures
- **Microempresa Familiar** — Home-based micro-business (simplest)
- **EIRL (Empresa Individual de Responsabilidad Limitada)** — Single-owner LLC
- **SpA (Sociedad por Acciones)** — Simplified corporation (most popular)
- **Ltda** — LLC equivalent
- **SA** — Corporation

### Costs
- Inicio de Actividades: Free (online via SII)
- SpA formation: CLP $50,000-$200,000 (~$50-$200 USD) online
- Patente: Varies by municipality and capital

### Tax Obligations
- **IVA**: 19% VAT
- **Primera Categoría**: 27% corporate income tax
- **PPM (Pagos Provisionales Mensuales)**: Monthly provisional tax payments
- **Electronic invoicing**: Mandatory via SII

## Costa Rica

### Requirements
1. **Cédula Jurídica** — Business ID from Registro Nacional
2. **Inscripción Tributaria** — Tax registration with Ministerio de Hacienda
3. **Patente Municipal** — Municipal operating license
4. **CCSS Registration** — Social security for employees (Caja Costarricense del Seguro Social)
5. **INS Registration** — Workers' comp insurance (Instituto Nacional de Seguros)
6. **Health Permit** — Ministerio de Salud for food/health businesses

### Tax Obligations
- **IVA**: 13% VAT
- **Income tax**: 5-30% progressive
- **CCSS**: Employer 26.33% + Employee 10.67% of payroll
- **SINPE Móvil**: Code "06" required on electronic invoices for SINPE payments (Sept 2025)

## Peru

### Requirements
1. **RUC** — Tax ID from SUNAT (sunat.gob.pe)
2. **SUNARP Registration** — Public registry (sunarp.gob.pe)
3. **Municipal License** — Operating license from municipality
4. **ESSALUD** — Health insurance for employees
5. **ONP or AFP** — Pension system for employees

### Business Structures
- **Persona Natural con Negocio** — Sole proprietor
- **EIRL** — Single-owner LLC
- **SAC (Sociedad Anónima Cerrada)** — Closed corporation (most common for SMBs)

### Tax Obligations
- **IGV**: 18% VAT
- **Renta**: 29.5% corporate income tax
- **RMT (Régimen MYPE Tributario)**: Simplified for small businesses (< 1700 UIT annual)
