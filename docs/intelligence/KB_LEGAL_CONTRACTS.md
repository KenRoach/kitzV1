- Electronic signature capability
- Document storage and management
- Shareable review links

DOCUSIGN -- ELECTRONIC SIGNATURE & CONTRACT MANAGEMENT:

PRODUCT OVERVIEW:
DocuSign is the world's #1 electronic signature platform with 1.5+ million customers.
It provides legally binding e-signatures, contract lifecycle management (CLM), and
intelligent agreement management (IAM). DocuSign signatures are legally valid in
180+ countries including all LATAM nations.

KEY FEATURES:
1. Electronic Signatures (eSignature):
   - Send documents for signature via email
   - Multiple signers, signing order, and roles
   - In-person signing on tablets/phones
   - Bulk send for mass document signing
   - Templates for frequently used documents
   - Mobile signing (iOS, Android)
   - Audit trail with timestamps and IP addresses

2. Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM):
   - Contract creation and collaboration
   - Approval workflows
   - Contract repository and search
   - Renewal reminders and alerts
   - Analytics and reporting

3. Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM):
   - AI-powered contract analysis
   - Risk identification
   - Obligation tracking
   - Data extraction from existing contracts

PRICING:
- Personal: $10/month -- 5 docs/month, 1 user
- Standard: $25/user/month -- unlimited docs
- Business Pro: $40/user/month -- advanced features, payment collection
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

LEGAL VALIDITY IN LATAM:
- Mexico: Valid under Ley de Firma Electronica Avanzada (LFEA)
- Colombia: Valid under Ley 527 de 1999 (Comercio Electronico)
- Argentina: Valid under Ley 25.506 (Firma Digital)
- Panama: Valid under Ley 51 de 2008 (Firmas Electronicas)
- Chile: Valid under Ley 19.799 (Documento Electronico)
- Brazil: Valid under MP 2.200-2/2001
- NOTE: Some documents still require notarization in LATAM

API & DEVELOPER INFO:
- REST API: Full CRUD on envelopes, templates, accounts
- eSignature API: Send, sign, manage documents programmatically
- Webhooks (Connect): Real-time notifications on signing events
- SDKs: Node.js, Python, PHP, Java, C#, Ruby
- Quickstart guides and sandbox environment
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- 400+ pre-built integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.)

- API enables KITZ to embed signing flows in its platform

- Pricing may be steep for micro-businesses -- Documenso (open-source)
  is the recommended alternative for cost-sensitive users
- Key use case: KITZ users sending service contracts via WhatsApp
  with DocuSign links for immediate signing
